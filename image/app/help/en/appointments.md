# Appointments — Help

Whole-day appointments with calendar view, ICS export and sprint capacity integration.

## Creating an appointment

Use the inline **+ Add** form on the appointments list or click **+ New Appointment**:

| Field | Notes |
|-------|-------|
| **Title** | Required |
| **From / To** | Whole-day range — end date can equal start date |
| **Type** | Training / Conference / Team Event / Business Trip / Other |
| **Note** | Optional free text |

## Calendar view

Click **Calendar** to see a monthly view. Navigate months with **‹** and **›**.

## ICS export

Click **Export ICS** to download calendar entries. Configure export profiles in **Settings → ICS Export Profiles** to customise filename patterns and field mappings.

## Sprint capacity

Appointments of type **Training**, **Conference** and **Business Trip** are counted as blocked days in the Sprint capacity planner. Configure which types are blocked in **Settings → Sprints**.
