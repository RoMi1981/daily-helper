# Notes — Help

Long-form notes with a monospace editor, in-note search and optional encryption.

## Creating a note

Click **+ New Note** and enter a **Subject** (required) and **Body**.

## Editing

Open a note and double-click the body, or click **Edit**.  
The editor auto-saves when focus leaves the form.

## In-note search

Use the search bar at the top of the detail view to highlight matching terms. Navigate matches with **Enter** / **Shift+Enter**.

## Encryption

Check **Encrypt body** when creating or editing. The body is encrypted at rest and only readable after entering the passphrase. Requires a `SECRET_KEY` environment variable to be set.

## Settings

Go to **Settings → Notes** to configure:
- **Scroll position** — where the cursor lands when opening a note (start / end)
- **Line numbers** — toggle the line-number gutter in the editor
