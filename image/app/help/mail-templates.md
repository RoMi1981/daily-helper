# Mail Templates — Help

Reusable email templates with one-click copy and optional EML download.

## Creating a template

Click **+ New Template** and fill in:

| Field | Notes |
|-------|-------|
| **Name** | Required — shown in the list |
| **To** | Optional, comma-separated addresses |
| **CC** | Optional, comma-separated addresses |
| **Subject** | Optional |
| **Body** | Full email body |

## Using a template

Click **Copy** on a template card to copy the formatted body to clipboard.  
Click **EML** to download a `.eml` file that opens directly in your email client with To, CC and Subject pre-filled.
