# Runbooks — Help

Ordered step-by-step procedures with a session checklist and progress tracking.

## Creating a runbook

1. Click **+ New Runbook**
2. Enter a **Title** and optional **Description**
3. Add steps with **+ Add Step** — each step has a title and optional body
4. Reorder steps with the ↑ ↓ buttons

## Running a runbook

Open a runbook and work through the steps:
- Check off each step using the checkbox — progress bar updates automatically
- Expand a step to see its body (commands, notes)
- Click **Copy** to copy the step body to clipboard
- Click **Reset** to uncheck all steps (uses `sessionStorage` — resets on browser close)

## Editing

Click **Edit** on the runbook detail page to modify title, description or steps.
