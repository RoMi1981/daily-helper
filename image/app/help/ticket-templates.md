# Ticket Templates — Help

Reusable ticket / issue templates with one-click copy.

## Creating a template

Click **+ New Template** and fill in:

| Field | Notes |
|-------|-------|
| **Name** | Required — shown in the list |
| **Description** | Optional short summary shown below the name |
| **Body** | Full ticket body — steps to reproduce, acceptance criteria, etc. |

## Using a template

Click **Copy** on a template card to copy the body to clipboard, then paste it into your ticketing system.
